General Instructions

CDs/Floppies are not allowed to be brought into the premises. Using Mobile Phones in the Company premises is restricted. Associates who have been provided by the company and Managers and above are permitted to use for their communication. Any personal belongings like books etc. can be brought in only after completing due formalities at the security. No material belonging to the company can be taken out without the approval of the concerned authority.

Dress Code

All Associates will follow , the Dress- Code policy laid down below. All Associates working in Software Division of Elico Ltd will come to work on all weekdays, strictly in formal clothing. Associate however, can come to work on weekends (Saturday & Sunday) in casual clothing (Eg: T-Shirts not allowed) appearance being neat and presentable. On all days of the week, irrespective of whether in formal or casual clothing, all associates will come in proper footwear (SHOES ONLY). For all Female associates, the above does not apply, however, the dress appearance will be neat and presentable. Proper footwear will be used. (SLIPPERS OF ANY KIND WILL NOT BE ALLOWED)
